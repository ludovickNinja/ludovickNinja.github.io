Used to scale the weight of a ring when changing the diamond size. For example, if a ring currently uses 1.2 mm diamonds and they want to upgrade to 2.0 mm stones, enter the current diamond size, target diamond size, band width, band thickness, and current weight. The tool will estimate the new weight and indicate whether the band thickness needs to be adjusted to maintain proper proportions.
